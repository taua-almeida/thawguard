How schedules work

A recurring schedule names freeze coverage that repeats, so nobody has to remember the freeze.

  1. 1
    One branch, unique name

    A schedule targets exactly one repository branch; its name is unique per branch.

  2. 2
    Explicit timezone

    The timezone is stored by name, so the schedule's freeze times will be interpreted in that zone — including across daylight saving shifts.

  3. 3
    Starts paused

    A new schedule is paused and does not freeze anything. Deleting it later is a single audited action.
